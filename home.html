WHAT IS CHESSMATE

We created a system that will employ a robot arm to play chess with a human.

  • A RealSense camera used by the robot arm to understand the state of the chessboard. After understanding the state, it decides the best movement and play accordingly.
  • The robot arm act as though it is competing with other players while playing.
  • The robot arm plays chess with humans while expressively making gestures like thinking, examining, hesitating, shows of satisfaction and bewilderment, breathing, etc.

It acts in the manner of a person. Robot-to-human nonverbal emotional expression will be applied to competitive play!

FEATURES

TO PROJECT ONESELF INTO THE FUTURE OF ART AND ENTERTAINMENT

Computer Vision Integrated

Predict the chessboard state and human state and deliver that to the main control package. Process the current image coming from the camera and understand the game state.

Artificial Intelligence Utilized

Sends the game state to the chess engine and gets the next movement. Use Stockfish to decide the next chess move.

Robatics Suited for Unique Design

Pick and place algorithm will be prepared for the robot arm in the real world as a base case. Interface for controlling the robot arm with predetermined motion schemes

Human-Robot Interaction Collaborated with SCM Hong Kong City University

Decide robot gestures according to the current score. Create pressure/interactiveness on the opponent if he/she is losing or winning according to the current game score,with the game state
